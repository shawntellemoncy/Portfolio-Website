My name is Shawntelle Marie Moncy and I am a journalism student at Eastern Washington University. I chose the major journalism because I love to write, writing is like an art to me and I love to portray my style and personality through my writing. My goal through life is to continually impress others and continually impress myself.

After graduation I would either like to join the Peace Corp and then go to graduate school for broadcasting in New York, where I’d like to live in the future. Or just go straight to graduate school. Why broadcasting? My favorite part of journalism through my experiences in classes here at Eastern and on “The Easterner” student newspaper, is although I love to write, my favorite part is simply interviewing and communicating with my interviewees. I like to make people feel comfortable and make sure that their even having fun talking to me. I’ve realized that that’s not how an interview is supposed to go in the newspaper world, so I’m having to hold back that temptation in the mean time. But in broadcast, I get to do just that! I would love to do live interviews or talk about subjects with other news peers and have fun with it! To work for “The Daily Show”, “Evening Magazine” or something of that sort would be a dream job to me.
